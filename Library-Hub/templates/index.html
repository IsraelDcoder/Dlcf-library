What this library is

It’s a reliable place where churches collect and share study materials — PDFs, eBooks, sermons, audio and video — so people can learn and grow at their own pace.

Features that matter

Read & study

Open PDFs and eBooks in the browser with clear navigation and saved progress.

Listen to teachings

Stream sermons and talks with reliable playback and simple controls.

Watch videos

Host teaching series and classes, organized by series and topic.

Manage with confidence

Role-based access, activity logs, and bulk upload tools for your team.

Live Streaming & Message Archiving

Stream messages and teachings live inside the library. Sessions appear as “Live Now”, are automatically saved, and recordings are categorized and published for on-demand listening or download.
